Asghar, S., Patani, S., and Menon, N. "Exploring Adoption Trends and Usage Patterns of Zero-Emission Vehicles: An MDCEV Model Approach" ASCE International Conference on Transportation and Development (ICTD), Glendale, AZ., 2025.

This presentation discusses the adoption trends and usage patterns of zero-emission vehicles using a Multiple Discrete-Continuous Extreme Value (MDCEV) modeling approach with data from the 2019 California Vehicle Survey.
