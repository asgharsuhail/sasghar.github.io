Asghar, S., and Kadali, B. R., (2023). "Assessing the Impact of Anxiety and Social Norms on Travel Behaviour in Hilly Regions" 102nd Annual Meeting of the Transportation Research Board, Washington, D.C., 2023.

This poster presents findings on how anxiety and social norms affect travel behavior in hilly regions, using data collected from Jammu & Kashmir, India.
