Asghar, S., Kadali, B.R., Menon, N., (2024). "Exploring Travel Anxiety in Hilly Regions: The Role of Travel Attitudes and Perception" ASCE International Conference on Transportation and Development (ICTD), Atlanta, GA., 2024.

This poster explores the role of travel attitudes and perception in shaping travel anxiety in hilly regions, contributing to the understanding of how psychological factors influence mode choice in challenging terrain environments.
