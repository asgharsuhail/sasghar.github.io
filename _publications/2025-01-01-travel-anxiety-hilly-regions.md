This study examines how anxiety, social norms, and companionship influence travel behavior in the hilly regions of Jammu and Kashmir, India. Using Structural Equation Modeling (SEM), the research explores the complex interplay between psychological and social factors that shape mode choice decisions in challenging terrain environments.
